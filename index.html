Atatürk Tribute

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

Here are some facts about His Excellency:

  • Mustafa Kemal Atatürk was, per Wikipedia, a Turkish army officer, revolutionary, and founder of the Republic of Turkey
  • After establishing the Republic of Turkey, Atatürk became its first president, serving from 1923 to his death in 1938.
  • Before his rise as a succesful politician, Atatürk was a legendary military officer in the Ottoman Empire, eventually becoming an esteemed general during WWI.
  • After the Ottoman Empire's ruin, Atatürk led the Turkish National Movement in the Turkish War of Independence.
  • After defeating the Allies of World War One and eventually won the Turkish war of Indepence.
  • Atatürk then embarked upon a program of political, economic, and cultural reforms.
  • He strove to turn his country into a modern, secular nation-state.
  • Under his leadership, thousands of new schools were built, primary education was made free and compulsory, and women were given equal civil and political rights, while the burden of taxation on peasants was reduced.
  • His government also carried out an extensive policy of Turkification.
  • The principles of Atatürk's reforms, upon which modern Turkey was established, are referred to as Kemalism.
  • His achievements in Turkey are an enduring monument to Atatürk.
